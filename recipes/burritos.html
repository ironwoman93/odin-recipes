Burritos

Description

Burritos are a delicious and easy meal option. This recipe is vegan.

Ingredients

  • 1 package uncooked tortillas
  • 2 packages chorizo seitan
  • 1 cup cooked rice of choice
  • cilantro
  • lime
  • 1 can black or pinto beans
  • ripe avocados
  • salsa of choice

Steps

  1. Cook chorizo seitan according to package directions
  2. While seitan is cooking, cook tortillas according to package directions
  3. Mix cilantro and lime juice into cooked rice
  4. Prepare burritos, topping seitan and rice with sliced avocados, beans, and salsa
