Chia Seed Pudding

Description

Chia seed pudding is a delicious and nutritous meal often eaten as breakfast or a snack. One of the great things about chia seed pudding is that, because it has an unobstrusive taste, you can include a wide variety of toppings depending on your preferences.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup chia seeds
  • 4 cups plant milk of choice
  • toppings of choice

Steps

  1. Add plant milk and chia seeds to bowl
  2. Whisk well until plant milk and chia seeds are fully incorporated.
  3. Wait several hours, then whisk pudding well again.
  4. Wait several more hours, then scoop and serve with your favorite toppings.
