Banana Bread

Description

Banana bread is a favorite American dessert and snack item. This banana bread is a healthy vegan alternative to sugary breads that doesn't skimp on taste.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 1/3 cup eurythritol
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon sea salt
  • 1/3 cup applesauce
  • 4 overripe bananas
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • cacao nibs as desired

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Mix together dry ingredients and set aside
  3. Mix together wet ingredients, fully incorporating and mashing bananas
  4. Slowly add dry ingredients to wet ingredients, stirring as you add. Mix just until incorporated.
  5. Pour batter into loaf pan and bake for about 50 minutes
